Golf Betting Strategy

Course History Matters

Players who perform well at specific courses tend to repeat. Always check 5-year history at the venue before placing outright bets.

Strokes Gained is King

Ignore traditional stats. Strokes Gained: Approach, Tee-to-Green, and Around-the-Green are the strongest predictors of tournament performance.

Weather Windows

Tee time draws matter. In windy conditions, the early/late wave advantage can be worth 2-3 strokes. Factor weather into matchup bets.

Value in Longshots

Golf has more variance than any major sport. Top-20 and Top-40 bets on 60/1+ players offer the best expected value long-term.
