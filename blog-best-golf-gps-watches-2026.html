Best Golf GPS Watches 2026: Garmin vs Apple Watch vs Samsung

Published March 2, 2026 · 13.Golf · 8 min read

Why Use a Golf GPS Watch

A golf GPS watch provides instant yardages to the front, center, and back of greens, hazard distances, and shot tracking — all on your wrist. No more searching for yardage markers or using a phone app. The best golf watches also track swing metrics, suggest clubs, and map every shot for post-round analysis.

1. Garmin Approach S70 — Best Dedicated Golf Watch

Price: $699 | Buy on Amazon

The S70 is the undisputed king of golf GPS watches. 43,000+ preloaded courses, full-color CourseView maps, PlaysLike distance (adjusts for elevation), and Virtual Caddie club suggestions. The 47mm AMOLED display is gorgeous and readable in sunlight.

  • 43,000+ courses preloaded
  • Virtual Caddie with AI club recommendations
  • Wind speed and direction overlay
  • Swing metrics with Garmin CT10 sensors
  • 20-hour GPS battery life

2. Apple Watch Ultra 2 — Best Smartwatch for Golf

Price: $799 | Buy on Amazon

With apps like Golfshot, Hole19, or SwingU, the Apple Watch Ultra becomes a capable golf GPS. The always-on 49mm display is the largest available. Best for golfers who want a premium daily smartwatch that also plays golf.

  • Best daily smartwatch with golf capability
  • Requires third-party golf app ($30-$100/yr)
  • Excellent fitness and health tracking
  • Cellular connectivity
  • Not as golf-specific as Garmin

3-4. Samsung Galaxy Watch Ultra and Bushnell ION Edge

Samsung Galaxy Watch Ultra: $649 | Buy on Amazon — Great Android alternative to Apple Watch. Smart Caddie app by Samsung provides course maps and shot tracking. Best for Android users.

Bushnell ION Edge: $199 | Buy on Amazon — Budget-friendly dedicated golf GPS. 38,000+ courses, auto course recognition, shot distance measurement. Best value under $200.
